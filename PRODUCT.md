# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Operations analysts and data coordinators who regularly receive Excel exports from multiple teams. They work under delivery pressure and need to import, inspect, and combine datasets quickly without losing trust in row counts or column meaning.

## Product Purpose
DataFlow helps users turn raw workbook exports into a consolidated, analysis-ready table. On the import page, success means users can upload files, map and rename relevant columns, and append data with clear feedback about parse status, warnings, and resulting totals.

## Brand Personality
Clear, reliable, calm. The interface should feel focused and competent, reducing cognitive load during repetitive data preparation work.

## Anti-references
Avoid flashy marketing aesthetics, novelty dashboard effects, and decorative interactions that compete with task completion. Avoid visual noise that obscures status, progress, or data quality signals.

## Design Principles
1. Keep users oriented: preserve clear status, counts, and next steps at each stage.
2. Prioritize task throughput: make upload, mapping, and append actions obvious and low-friction.
3. Build trust through transparency: expose warnings and row/column impact without drama.
4. Reduce visual decision fatigue: emphasize hierarchy and consistency over ornament.
5. Respect operational reality: support keyboard usage, repeat imports, and graceful recovery from errors.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA contrast and interaction clarity. Preserve keyboard navigation across upload, dialog, and data grid flows. Keep visible focus states and honor reduced-motion preferences.
